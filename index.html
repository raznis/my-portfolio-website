About Me

My background is in computer science research. My Ph.D. was focused on automated planning, multi-agent systems, and algorithmic game-theory. While at Yahoo research, I worked on ad click-prediction modeling, and was responsible for online learning pipelines with a direct effect on revenue and >100M users. Since 2016, I'm at General Motors, where I have been hard at work building an awesome team of developers, system engineers, integration engineers, and SW validation engineers responsible for Simulation, Integration, and Testing efforts for the Ultracruise project and more.
More information and a full list of publications (with slides) can be found on my personal homepage (http://raznissim.wix.com/raznis).
Specialties: Recommender systems, Click-Prediction, Artificial Intelligence, Automated Planning Systems, Multi-Agent Planning, Mechanism Design, AV simulation, AV validation/testing, SW validation/automation.
